Precision and Performance comparison between EtherCAT and PWM:

EtherCAT: High precision and performance, especially in systems requiring precise synchronization and real-time control.

PWM: Provides sufficient precision for basic control tasks but may not be as suitable for applications with stringent timing requirements.

EtherCAT: Commonly used in high-performance industrial automation, motion control, robotics, and applications requiring precise synchronization and real-time communication.

CAN: Wide use in automotive applications, industrial automation, and distributed control systems. Well-suited for applications where moderate-speed communication is acceptable.
